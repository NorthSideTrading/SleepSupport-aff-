When to See a Professional

These products are not medical treatments. Consult a healthcare provider if you experience:

  • Sleep apnea symptoms (breathing interruptions during sleep)
  • Severe or persistent snoring despite trying non-pill solutions
  • Daytime fatigue that affects daily activities
  • Jaw pain or dental issues when using oral devices

A sleep study may be recommended to identify underlying conditions requiring professional treatment.
