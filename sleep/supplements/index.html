Supplement FAQ

Melatonin vs non-melatonin options?

Non-melatonin options like magnesium and L-theanine support relaxation without affecting natural melatonin production. They may be preferred for long-term use.

When should I take sleep supplements?

Most supplements work best when taken 30-60 minutes before bedtime. Follow product-specific instructions and maintain consistent timing for best results.

Do supplements interact with medications?

Some supplements can interact with medications, especially blood thinners, antidepressants, and other sleep aids. Always consult your healthcare provider before starting any supplement.

How long should I test a supplement?

Most supplements require 1-2 weeks of consistent use to evaluate effectiveness. Keep a sleep diary to track changes in sleep quality and duration.
