Frequently Asked Questions

Melatonin vs non-melatonin options?
Non-melatonin options like magnesium support relaxation without affecting natural melatonin production. They may be preferred for long-term use.
When should I take sleep supplements?
Most supplements work best when taken 30-60 minutes before bedtime. Follow product-specific instructions and maintain consistent timing.
Do supplements interact with medications?
Some supplements can interact with medications. Always consult your healthcare provider before starting any supplement regimen.
How long should I test a supplement?
Most supplements require 1-2 weeks of consistent use to evaluate effectiveness. Keep a sleep diary to track changes.
